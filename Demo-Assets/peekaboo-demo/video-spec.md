# Codexify Peekaboo Demo

## Intent

Silent, 35-second visual peek at a populated Codexify guest-facing workspace. It shows the product at rest, a compact return-to-work conversation, the dense sidebar, the deliberate sidebar scroll, and a second focused conversation.

This is a visual impression, not a tutorial, benchmark, narrated film, collaboration demo, or comprehensive feature tour.

## Capture sequence

| Time | View | Motion |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00–0:09 | `What changed since Tuesday?` near the top of the sidebar | Gentle fade in; hold on the settled transcript. |
| 0:09–0:14 | Gallery surface | Show the three fictional cached image assets without overlays. |
| 0:14–0:19 | Documents surface | Show the three fictional cached document cards. |
| 0:19–0:31 | `Untangle the onboarding flow` | Select the lower populated thread; the sidebar settles on the lower context. |
| 0:31–0:35 | Final workspace hold | Small ease-out; end on the populated workspace. |

## Technical target

- 1920×1080
- 30 fps
- 35 seconds
- H.264 MP4
- no audio track
- no cursor emphasis, voiceover, music, connector, memory, or collaboration claims
