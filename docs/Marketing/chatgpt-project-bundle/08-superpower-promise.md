# Method 5: Give Them a Superpower

Codexify superpower:

Cognitive continuity under complexity.

Expanded promise:

Codexify helps you keep your thoughts, documents, projects, workflows, and AI interactions connected across time without surrendering ownership.

## Before / After Capability Shift

| Before | After |
|---|---|
| I lose context across chats | I build durable project continuity |
| My documents sit outside the conversation | My documents become retrievable working context |
| I rely on scattered SaaS tools | I operate a local-first intelligence layer |
| I cannot tell what the system did | I inspect truth surfaces and proof artifacts |
| My workflows live in my head | My workflows become encodable and reusable |
| My AI usage feels fragmented | My AI practice becomes infrastructure |

## Copy Rule

Superpower language must always map to specific, supportable workflow behavior.
