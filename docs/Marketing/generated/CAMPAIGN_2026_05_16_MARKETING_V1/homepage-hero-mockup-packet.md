- Bottom: compressed vertical spine with 3 primary nodes and one proof-callout chip anchored near node 2.

Safe claim used:

Caution claims avoided:

## Claim-Risk Notes

- Keep all hero copy tied to safe claims: local-first supported posture, continuity, retrieval, inspectability, and evidence-linked governance.
- Treat provider breadth, connector maturity, command-bus details, and control-plane semantics as caution-only and keep them out of headline/subheadline language.
- Avoid absolute terms such as "guaranteed," "always," "never lose context," and any wording that implies autonomous operation.
