# 01 Product Identity and Public Story

## Core public frame

`Current`: Codexify is a local-first AI workspace for ongoing project thinking, making, and recall.

`Philosophy`: It should be described as a continuity substrate, not just a chat window. The product story is about preserving project, thread, and document continuity inside a user-owned working environment.

## Public architecture-to-story translation

`Current`:
- Local-first AI workspace
- Project and thread continuity
- User-owned memory posture
- Inspectable runtime truth
- Proof-before-promise release discipline

`Philosophy`:
- Codexify should feel like a place where work accumulates with lineage, not a stateless prompt terminal.
- Continuity matters because users return to projects, documents, and unfinished thought over time.

## What the story can safely emphasize

`Current`:
- Threads, projects, and documents are meaningful continuity surfaces.
- The runtime is observable enough to distinguish health, queueing, retrieval, and completion states.
- Retrieval is grounded working context, not mystical omniscience.

`Philosophy`:
- The system is designed to help people think with their own materials rather than outsource all cognition into a black box.

## Mythic framing versus factual claims

`Philosophy`:
- Mythic framing can shape atmosphere, naming, and visual rhetoric.
- It may suggest archive, observatory, continuity field, or artifact vault.

`Current`:
- Mythic framing is not evidence of a shipped capability.
- Public copy must keep factual claims separable from poetic framing.

## Suggested public language patterns

- `Current`: "Codexify is a local-first AI workspace built around projects, threads, documents, and inspectable runtime behavior."
- `Current`: "The current supported beta path is the local Docker Compose stack."
- `Philosophy`: "Codexify is designed as a continuity substrate for ongoing work."
- `Current`: "It favors user-owned context, visible system state, and proof-driven claims."

## Prohibited public language patterns

- "Codexify understands everything about you automatically."
- "Codexify runs everywhere the same way today."
- "Codexify already ships autonomous delegation/federation/graph intelligence as a public release promise."
- "The website itself is the Codexify runtime."
