## Note

This index tracks report-only artifacts stored in `docs/collaborators/zac/reports/`.

Entries in this index are not tasks. They are not approvals. They are not release truth. They are learning records that may or may not lead to future proposals.

## Intake Rule

A report may raise a possible future proposal. A report does not itself authorize implementation.

Medium or high-risk follow-up must go through:

1. Resonant's constraints.
2. The correct Codexify task lane (Standard, Architecture-Impact, or Docs-only).
3. A separate proposal using `../proposal-template.md`.

Low-risk observations may become standard tasks if Resonant agrees, but the report itself is not a task. Do not treat a report row as a work item.
