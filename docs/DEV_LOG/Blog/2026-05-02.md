What stood out most is that this was structural work, not celebratory work. So the day's real achievement was not "we shipped Pi" or "we launched public beta." It was that the system became more truthful about what is ready, what is planned, and what still needs validation.

That leaves the next move pretty clear. The day made the system more legible. The next step is making it operationally undeniable.
